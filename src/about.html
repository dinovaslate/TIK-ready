    About Slippy Dripp


    Slippy Dripp is an online shoe store that resells high-quality original shoes at the best prices. Our shop guarantees the authenticity of the products we sell, every item we sell goes through our proprietary multi-step verification process with our team of expert authenticators. In our shoe store, you can find shoes from Nike, Adidas, Puma, and Rebook. These shoes have various sizes and color variants that suit the customers' personal preferences. Whether it’s pre-release, regionally limited, or even the ones that are sold out in other mediocre stores – our non-stop shoes supply from over 200+ official product distributors allows you to easily secure those hard-to-find, coveted items. The reason our store is different and better than the other stores by a fine margin is that not only do we bring you the best quality shoes but Slippy Dripp also guarantees the speed in bringing the goods you ordered quickly, safely, and precisely, hence why our store tagline is "Get your drip in a blip". This shop was created by group 2 whose members include Ali Abdurrahman, Arkan Fadhil Kautsar, Fadlan Hadif Rafidhan and Haekal Alexander Dinova.

    History of us



    This fabulous group of individuals was brought together for the sole purpose of completing a group assignment in the Information and Technology class by our teacher, Mr. Sukron. The assignment was given on April 7th with a deadline of April 28th. The task given to us was to make an online shopping website using HTML, CSS, and Javascript, which have been taught to us this past academic year. We chose to sell shoes on our online shopping website. The idea was to make a simple yet sophisticated and stylish website design. This store would not have been possible but for the help, we got from the people among us. By making this project, we hope that our web programming knowledge and skill will be improved through online literacy and discussion with one another. Thank you and we hope you're going to like and make good of our website. Who knows what good can this website project bring?
